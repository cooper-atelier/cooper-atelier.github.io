The Cosmic Ricercar

Almost aligned arms

即将校准的臂旋

Slender curves begin to part

弧光渐次绽开

The stars obey a winding call

缠绕的召引

In loops that hesitate

犹疑的环套

The heart of stirring

搅动的心

Blooming helix

螺旋漫溢

The form forgets its symmetry

忘却对称

Perpendicular explosions

垂直的焰火
