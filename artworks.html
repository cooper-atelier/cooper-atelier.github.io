The Cosmic Ricercar

In music, a fugue is based on a concise theme, initially presented by one voice, and subsequently developed continuously through imitation by other voices. The Cosmic Ricercar, drawing upon the diverse forms of nebular genesis, uses spiral curves as the theme's trajectory, incorporating noise and time-evolving distortions to drive the initial form through constant variation and evolution, ultimately returning in a cycle.

Neo-Sino Mysticism Series

The series aims to construct abstract mystic models using images and symbols, refining their parameters to explore deeper meanings. Through visual art, this series explores and presents the application and significance of mysticism in contemporary society. By fusing ancient symbols with modern geometric forms, it creates a new visual language. The abstract mystic painting models will showcase the relationships and interactions between various mystical elements, allowing viewers to perceive the pathways and nodes of energy flow between symbols. By adjusting various "parameters" of the models, the works attempt to explore and reveal the effects and significance of different mystical elements, examining their influence on broader societal concepts.

Reimagining Series

This collection is devoted to reinterpreting various traditional Chinese mystical elements. By substituting traditional symbols with modern motifs and reconfiguring their arrangements and combinations, it aims to evoke fresh meanings within a contemporary visual context. Additionally, through the creation of new narratives, the series seeks to envision novel functions and roles for these mystical symbols within an imaginative and boundless setting.
